About Divine Echos

A community built on the foundation of music, gaming, and genuine connections.

Our Story

Divine Echos started as a small group of friends who shared a passion for music and gaming. What began as casual jam sessions and game nights quickly grew into a thriving community of 208+ members from around the world.

🌟

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a place where they can be themselves. Our mission is to create a safe, welcoming space where music heals, gaming unites, and friendships last forever.

💜

Our Values

Respect, inclusivity, and positivity are at the core of everything we do. We maintain a strict no-toxicity policy to ensure everyone feels comfortable and valued.

Community Guidelines

SUPREME DISCORD RULES — Read carefully to keep our community safe and welcoming for everyone.

01
👑

Respect & Behavior

  • Treat all members with respect.
  • No toxicity, hate speech, racism, threats, or bullying.
02
🛡️

Content Rules

  • NSFW, illegal, violent, or disturbing content is strictly prohibited.
  • No fake news or misleading information.
03
📢

Spam & Promotion

  • No spamming, emojis flooding, or mass mentions.
  • Self-promotion only in allowed channels with permission.
04
🔒

Privacy & Safety

  • Do not share personal information (yours or others).
  • No DM advertising or harassment.
05
🎭

Identity & Impersonation

  • Impersonating staff, bots, or members is not allowed.
06
🎙️

Voice & Chat Rules

  • No mic spamming, soundboards abuse, or voice trolling.
  • Follow channel topics at all times.
07
⚡

Staff Authority

  • Respect staff decisions.
  • Arguing with staff publicly may lead to punishment.
08
🔨

Punishment System

  • Warning → Timeout → Mute → Kick → Ban
  • Severe rule breaks = Instant ban.
09
🧠

Common Sense Rule

  • Anything not listed but harmful to the server is still punishable.
